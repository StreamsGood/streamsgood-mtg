Hyper Extended

hextended.com

Hyper Extended is a variant format comprised of the black bordered sets from Fallen Empires through Scourge.

  1. Ante cards are banned.
  2. Mana Burn is in effect.
  3. Wishes may retrieve cards from exile.
  4. Gold-Bordered cards are permitted, if otherwise legal.

Hyper Extended features a 20 card Restricted List.
